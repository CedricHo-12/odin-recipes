Fried Chicken

Description

Here you are looking at one of the world famous dishes that is enjoyed by people at every corner on this planet. The crunchiness of the outer layer combines with the tenderness of the meat is the best combo ever created by mankind. We will share the secret way that will help you experience the pleasure of eating this legendary dish

Ingredients

  • Drum sticks
  • Cooking oil
  • Garlic
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Crackers

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic using. If you don't have a crusher, chop the garlic repeatitively.
  2. Mix the minced garlic with the drum sticks
  3. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to add some flavour to the drum sticks
  4. Crack an egg into the mixture
  5. Put it aside for 30 minutes
  6. Add cooking oil into pan until it cover at least half of the drum sticks
  7. Heat the oil up and slowly put the drum sticks into the pan
  8. Let it fry for 4 minutes and flip the drumk sticks
  9. Let it fry for another 3 minutes
  10. Finally, turn the heat up for 30 seconds before turning off the heat
